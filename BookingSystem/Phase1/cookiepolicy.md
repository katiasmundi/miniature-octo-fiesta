# Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how the Booking System uses cookies and similar technologies.

## 1. What Are Cookies?
Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website.  
They are commonly used to make websites work efficiently and securely.

## 2. Cookies Used by the Booking System
The Booking System uses cookies for the following purposes:

### Essential Cookies
These cookies are required for the system to function properly and cannot be disabled.

Examples include:
- Session cookies used to keep users logged in
- Security-related cookies such as CSRF protection tokens

Without these cookies, the system cannot operate correctly.

### Functional Cookies
Functional cookies may be used to:
- Remember user preferences
- Improve usability and user experience

These cookies do not track users for marketing purposes.

## 3. Cookies Not Used
The Booking System does **not** use:
- Advertising cookies
- Tracking cookies
- Third-party analytics cookies

No personal data is shared with third-party services through cookies.

## 4. Cookie Consent
During registration or first use of the system, users are informed about the use of cookies.  
By continuing to use the system, users consent to the use of essential cookies.

Optional cookies are not used in this application.

## 5. Managing Cookies
Users can control or delete cookies through their browser settings.  
Disabling essential cookies may prevent the system from functioning correctly.

## 6. Changes to This Cookie Policy
This Cookie Policy may be updated if cookie usage changes.  
Any updates will be published on this page.

## 7. Contact Information
If you have questions regarding this Cookie Policy, please contact the system administrator.
